## Detailed Descriptions

### 🔹 Minimal
- **Philosophy**: Less is more. Only the essential information.
- **Best for**: Executive summaries, high-level overviews, slide decks
- **Visual**: Generous whitespace, large fonts, short labels
- **Text rule**: Truncate labels to 30 characters max; omit descriptions

### 🔸 Standard (Default)
- **Philosophy**: Balanced readability and information content.
- **Best for**: Documentation, team presentations, general use
- **Visual**: Comfortable spacing, readable fonts, moderate detail
- **Text rule**: Labels up to 60 characters; include brief descriptions

### 🔶 Detailed
- **Philosophy**: Comprehensive information with full context.
- **Best for**: Technical documentation, training materials, reference diagrams
- **Visual**: Tighter spacing, smaller fonts, rich annotations
- **Text rule**: Labels up to 120 characters; full descriptions and notes

### 🔷 Compact
- **Philosophy**: Maximum data in minimum space.
- **Best for**: Complex systems, wall posters, technical deep-dives
- **Visual**: Minimal whitespace, small fonts, dense layout
- **Text rule**: Labels up to 200 characters; all available information shown
